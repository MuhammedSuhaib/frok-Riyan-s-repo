Phulki Recipe

Ingredients

Prepare Phulki:

  • 1 Cup Moong daal (Yellow lentil) (soaked for 1 hour)
  • 2-3 cloves Lehsan (Garlic)
  • ½ tsp Lal mirch powder (Red chilli powder) or to taste
  • ½ tsp Himalayan pink salt or to taste
  • ½ Cup Water
  • ½ tsp Baking soda
  • Cooking oil for frying
  • Water as required
  • 1 tsp Himalayan pink salt

Prepare Creamy Yogurt:

  • 750g Dahi (Yogurt)
  • ½ Cup Olper’s Cream
  • ½ Cup Sugar or to taste

Prepare Gur Imli ki Chutney:

  • 1 Cup Gur (Jaggery)
  • 4 tbsp Imli pulp (Tamarind pulp)
  • ½ tsp Lal mirch powder (Red chilli powder) or to taste
  • 1 tsp Sonth powder (Ginger powder)
  • ¼ tsp Himalayan pink salt or to taste
  • ¼ tsp Lal mirch (Red chilli) crushed
  • 1 & ½ Cup Water
  • 2 tbsp Cornflour
  • 2-3 tbsp Water
  • Chaat masala

Directions

Prepare Phulki:

  1. In a blender, add yellow lentil, garlic, red chilli powder, pink salt, water & blend well.
  2. Take out in a bowl, add baking soda & whisk well until fluffy then transfer to a piping bag.
  3. In a wok, heat cooking oil, place a wire rack over hot oil & pipe out phulki batter over it & fry on medium flame until golden & crispy.
  4. In a large bowl, add water & pink salt & mix well.
  5. Add fried phulki, cover & let them soak until soft (15 minutes) then take out from water and squeeze gently to remove excess water & set aside.

Note: To check the consistency of batter, drop 1 tbsp of batter in water. If it sinks, beat again for 1-2 minutes. If batter floats, it's ready to use.

Prepare Creamy Yogurt:

  1. In a bowl, add yogurt, cream, sugar, whisk well until sugar is dissolved & set aside.

Prepare Gur Imli ki Chutney:

  1. In a saucepan, add jaggery, tamarind pulp, red chilli powder, ginger powder, pink salt, red chilli crushed & water, mix well & bring it to a boil & cook on medium flame for 4-5 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, add cornflour, water & mix well.
  3. Now add dissolved cornflour, mix well & cook until it thickens.
  4. Let it cool completely.

Assembling:

  1. In a serving dish, add phulkiyan, creamy yogurt, gur imli ki chutney, chaat masala & serve!
